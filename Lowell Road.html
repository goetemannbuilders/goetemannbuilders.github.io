    Lowell Road, Concord

    Nashawtuc Architects
    1998-1999

    Very engaged clients and Holly Cratzley challenged us to build one story additions an 3 sides of this antique Cape and add 3 gables to the 2nd floor while preserving finishes below. Each roof plane being a different pitch, each bedroom ceiling being cathedral style, we used an awful lot of string to make planes meet perfectly. Custom doors and millwork, new mechanical systems and finishes inside and out of the turret made us all better builders.
